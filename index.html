PC Truck Game Controls

Play truck simulator games on PC are fun and exciting, especially for those who enjoy driving big vehicles. To control the truck in these games, players usually use a simple set of keyboard keys. Understanding these controls can help you drive better and enjoy the game more.

Arrow Up ↑:

Move Forward The Arrow Up key is used to move the truck forward. Press and hold this key to accelerate and drive ahead.

Arrow Down ↓:

Reverse / Slow Down The Arrow Down key helps in slowing down the truck or moving it in reverse. It’s useful when you need to stop or back up.

Arrow Left ←:

Turn Left Use the Left Arrow key to steer the truck to the left. This helps in making left turns on roads or tight curves.

Arrow Right →:

Turn Right The Right Arrow key is used to steer the truck to the right, allowing for smooth right turns.

B Key: Brake

The "B" key works as the brake. Press it to stop the truck quickly or to reduce speed when needed. It’s especially useful when going downhill or avoiding obstacles.
